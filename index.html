About KJSCE Hackathon
The KJSCE hackathon 2016 saw the particiapation of all tech-savy people across Mumbai and we were in awe of the innovation ideas and brilliant products in display. The dedicated approach by the participants helped set a high standard for the comprition in its first year itself. Hackathon 2016 was held in KJSCE as a part of the annual technical fest, Abhiayntriki. This tradition will be followed and we will be hosting the Hackathon 2017 during Abhiyantriki 2017, a two day festival from 6th October to 7th October.
